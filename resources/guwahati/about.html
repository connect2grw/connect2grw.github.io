Guwahati Artisan Group


We are group of artisans from Assam, India practicing traditional cane and bamboo crafts. We have pooled our talents, energy and financial resources to create a self-reliant group that is able to take responsibility of all the spheres—design, production, sales and marketing. We want to provide our customers with exquisite and desirable handcrafted products. We want to work directly with our patrons and supporters and not depend upon middlemen or moneylenders.

You can buy our products at Pragjyotika Assam Craft Emporium stores in all the major cities in India, including—Chennai, Kolkata, New Delhi, and Mumbai.



Customization


We can customize a bulk order (minimum 100 pieces) for you. From logo printing to custom embossing we do it all. Turn around time from approval of proofs is about 4-5 months based on standard shipping methods. Extra charges may apply for expedited turn around.



Materials: Bamboo

Molded bamboo splits have been used to create the tray that you see here. A single dye is used to create the entire design.

The splits are reversed at the ends to work as handles.

Thin bamboos are used as side ends to hold all the splits together. The design is 100% green and it doesn’t use any adhesives or chemicals.
